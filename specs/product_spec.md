# CareerOS AI - Product Specification

## Project Name

CareerOS AI

## Tagline

The AI Operating System for Student Success

---

## Problem Statement

Students often struggle to navigate their academic and professional growth due to fragmented resources and lack of personalized guidance.

Common challenges include:

* Identifying which skills to learn
* Choosing meaningful projects
* Finding relevant hackathons
* Discovering scholarship opportunities
* Preparing for internships
* Building a structured career roadmap

Existing solutions address only individual parts of this journey rather than providing a unified system.

---

## Solution

CareerOS AI is a multi-agent platform built using Google ADK that helps students move from learning to employment through personalized guidance, planning, and opportunity discovery.

The system combines specialized AI agents, skills, MCP integrations, security controls, and evaluation mechanisms into a single workflow.

---

## Target Users

### Primary Users

* College students
* University students
* Early-career professionals

### Secondary Users

* Hackathon participants
* Scholarship applicants
* Open-source contributors

---

## Core Features

### Career Strategist Agent

Generates personalized learning and career roadmaps.

### Research-to-Project Agent

Converts research papers and technical content into practical project ideas and implementation plans.

### Hackathon Agent

Suggests hackathon opportunities, project ideas, and execution strategies.

### Scholarship Agent

Identifies scholarships and evaluates eligibility.

### Internship Agent

Analyzes resumes, identifies skill gaps, and provides internship readiness recommendations.

---

## Expected Outcomes

Users should be able to:

* Receive a personalized career roadmap
* Convert research into actionable projects
* Discover relevant opportunities
* Improve internship readiness
* Plan their professional growth more effectively
